About Us

Our History

Seams Creative was founded by Sandra, a passionate designer who has been sewing since childhood. Her love for creativity and fabric led her to build a business focused on bringing custom embroidery to life.

Today, Sandra designs and digitizes pictures and logos, which are stitched using a fleet of advanced embroidery machines. Every item is carefully crafted to meet each customer’s vision and specifications.

Seams Creative specializes in unique, personalized gifts such as wedding clocks, custom tote bags, logo apparel for businesses and clubs, baby blankets, bibs, and more. Every stitch tells a story—and we’re here to help you tell yours.
