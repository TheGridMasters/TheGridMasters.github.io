Digitizing

Digitizing is the first step in bringing your design to life through embroidery. We convert your image or logo into a digital embroidery file using expert software that maps each stitch precisely. Whether it’s a simple monogram or a complex logo, our team ensures it’s clean, balanced, and ready for stitching.
